Produce the executive summary as the ``summary`` field of the response contract.
Use this structure inside ``summary`` (markdown):

# {client_name} — Proposal Summary

## What they offered
One paragraph: the core scope, deliverables, and any unique angle.

## Price & terms
- Total: $...
- Payment schedule: ...
- Hidden costs flagged: ...

## Timeline
- Kickoff: ...
- Milestone 1: ...
- Go-live: ...

## Risks & open questions
- ...

## Recommended next move
One paragraph; concrete action with owner and deadline.

For ``next_recommended_tasks``, list 2-4 follow-ups (e.g. "schedule legal review",
"ask client about milestone 2 acceptance criteria"). Each should have a clear task,
suggested priority, and (when obvious) the plugin that could execute it.

For ``files_modified`` and other code-related contract fields, return [] — this is
a read-only summarization workflow and produces no code changes.
